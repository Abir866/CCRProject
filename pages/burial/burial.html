THE BURIAL YOU SHOULD KNOW

Want a natural burial?

Our conservation provied the green-burial service, If you actually have the demand for it. Please call us at 352-317-7307 to discuss your personal situation. In this page, we will simply introduce our service and the process of online booking. We trust it will save a lot of time for you. The main content of the process is a form you need to fill. Before you enter the form, there is an agreement you should sign. We trust this agreement can actually protect your right, so please read carefully.

If you would like to prepay for your burial, some funeral homes can include our cost in a plan with their services. There are other options as well, such as CDs payable upon death or dedicated savings accounts. Prepaying a funeral home is a choice some people make, but the most important thing to do is to make and share a plan. To understand the decisions involved in arranging a natural burial please review our Burial Planning Guide, look at our FAQ page, and/or give us a call.
